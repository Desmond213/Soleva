Our story

Striving for better in our nature

we set out to create an entirely new category of shoes inspired by natural materials, guided by an ethos to create better things in a better way. We've made a lot progress, but we're just getting started

OUR JOURNEY FROM NZ

arrow_left arrow_right

It's all part of making better things in a better way

Our story began in New Zealand in 2007, the passion project of former professional footballer Tim Brown

Disillusiioned with over branded shoes made with petroleum - based synthetics, he saw an oppotunity to do things better

Partnering with renewable expert Joey Zwillinger in 2014, the two set out to revolutionize the shoe industry

Better Things

We work with—not against—Mother Nature, favoring both recycled and natural materials like responsibly-sourced Merino wool, tree fiber, and sugarcane. Every choice we make is a step toward a world we could all feel better about living in.
